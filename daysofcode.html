100 Days of Code

I came across the idea of participating in the 100 days of coding challenge while doing inspiration research for my portfolio. I think this is a very simple way to improve my coding skills and builds on the concept of why I started this blog. Since I want to strengthen my fundamentals, for 100 days I will do atleast 1 Leetcode question of medium to hard difficulty and record it here. This is my plan for now. I also want to work on more projects so once I start them, I will include them as a supplement to the 1 question per day. Wish me luck!
